Completed as an NZIE Diploma capstone — a self-directed strategy built against a real charity's public mission, not commissioned or run by Make-A-Wish NZ.

The Brief

400 kids. One waitlist.

Make-A-Wish NZ's highest waitlist in recent history came down to inconsistent funding. Instead of leaning on guilt, I repositioned the Monthly Giving Programme as a transparent, high-value subscription — a logical fix, not a sympathy ask.

The Audience

Meet Mindful Markus

Every asset was written toward one persona — someone whose objections could actually be designed against.

"Mindful Markus"

42 · Senior Software Engineer · Auckland · German-born

Moved from Germany a decade ago for better work-life balance. Logic-first and detail-oriented — he wants to see exactly where his money goes before committing to anything recurring. Values Manaakitanga: caring for the community he's chosen to settle in.

The Approach

Built on the SPECH framework

Every messaging decision was pressure-tested from five angles before it made the cut.

S

Social

Markus scrolls Instagram and YouTube daily — the campaign fits his existing habits instead of interrupting them.

P

Political

Coming from Germany, he sees charities as filling gaps public healthcare can't — reframing the gift as shared responsibility.

E

Economic

A stable senior salary gives him room for $25/month, plus a professional network to bring others along.

C

Cultural

Bridges his German norm of trusted wish-granting charities with local Manaakitanga — a "best of both worlds" trust factor.

H

Historical

His own childhood memories, plus Make-A-Wish's long NZ track record, gave him the proof a logic-first donor needs.

Deliverables

A three-phase content funnel

Built to move Markus from cold awareness to a signed-up donor without ever feeling like a hard sell.

01

Awareness — Static Carousel

Posted twice weekly during commute hours. Frames the $25 gift as an efficient, low-admin choice — the soft ask.

02

Emotional — Video Ad

A 16-second video contrasting a sterile hospital soundscape with a granted wish's payoff — logic gives way to connection.

03

Urgent Closer — IG Story

Daily stories using AI-generated imagery, stripped to one visual and a direct sign-up link — the final nudge.

✓

Ethical Guardrail

No AI-generated children anywhere in the creative — a symbolic shooting star stood in instead, for a cause this sensitive.
